Who we are:

At NutriPaw, we are passionate about improving the lives of pets and the people who care for them. We understand that every pet is unique, with its own needs, habits, and personality. Our goal is to give pet owners the tools they need to better understand and support their animals every single day.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to simplify pet care through smart, accessible technology. By combining health tracking, behavioral insights, and collaborative features, we aim to make responsible pet ownership easier and more effective for everyone - from first-time pet owners to experienced caregivers.

What We Offer:

NutriPaw provides a centralized platform where you can create detailed profiles for each of your pets. Within these profiles, you can track a wide range of information, including:

  • Nutrition and feeding routines
  • Health updates, symptoms, and observations
  • Medicinal intakes
  • Behavioral patterns and changes over time
By documenting this information consistently, you gain valuable insights that help you recognize trends and respond proactively to your pet's needs.

Collaboration & Shared Care:

We know that pets are often cared for by more than one person. Whether it's family members in the same household or trusted pet sitters, communication can sometimes be a challenge. NutriPaw solves this by enabling shared access to pet profiles, ensuring that everyone involved has the same up-to-date information. This reduces misunderstandings, improves coordination, and ensures that your pet receives consistent and reliable care - even when you're not there.

Our Vision:

We envision a world where every pet owner has the knowledge and tools to provide the best possible care. By making pet health and behavior easier to track and understand, we hope to strengthen the bond between pets and their owners.
At NutriPaw, we're not just building a tool - we're creating a smarter, more connected way to care for the animals that mean so much to us.
